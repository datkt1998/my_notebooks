Matrix factorizations are widely used for Recommendation Systems. They are a great and easy starting point if you want to build a quick and easy solution to deliver great recommendations to your customers.

This article covers the steps needed to automate a BigQueryML Matrix Factorization recommendation pipeline. Including automatic handling of slot reservations to keep the costs at a minimum. As well as deploying the model to Cloud Run to make online recommendations.
